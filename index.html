Our Story

A Sanctuary Built for Nairobi's Pace

Waridi Paradise Hotel & Suites is a 4-star independent hotel nestled on Rose Avenue, off Argwings Kodhek Road in Kilimani — one of Nairobi's most vibrant neighbourhoods. With 54 thoughtfully designed rooms and suites across six floors, we blend contemporary African style with the comforts of home. From our heated pool and health club to our spacious kitchenette suites ideal for extended stays, every detail is crafted for the modern business and leisure traveller. Waridi means rose in Swahili — and like the flower, we strive to bring beauty, calm, and warmth to every stay.
